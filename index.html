STEPS Decision Aid Tool

This tool supports decision makers in planning and scaling Field Epidemiology Training Programs (FETP) by predicting programme uptake, performing cost–benefit analyses, and evaluating willingness‐to‐pay (WTP) for six key attributes (all in USD).

Introduction

  • Instructions: Step-by-step usage guide.
  • Attributes: Overview of six FETP attributes with reference levels and icons.
  • Inputs: Choose non-reference levels for discrete attributes; adjust two sliders (Cohort Size, Cost per Participant).
  • WTP Results: Shows extra value (in USD) for each non-reference level (including the continuous ones per +1 increment).
  • Predicted Program Uptake: A doughnut chart and summary reflect your chosen scenario.
  • Costs & Benefits: QALY scenario selection, summary table, bar chart, cost breakdown cards.
  • Scenarios: Save, load, or export multiple configurations to PDF.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Visit Attributes to see each attribute, its reference level, and a tooltip with explanations.
  2. Go to Inputs to pick only non-reference radio buttons for Training Level, Delivery Method, Accreditation, and Location. Adjust the two sliders for Cohort Size and Cost per Participant as desired.
  3. Click "Calculate & View Results" to see a modal with the predicted uptake percentage. The Predicted Program Uptake tab shows the resulting doughnut chart.
  4. The WTP Results chart displays the extra value for each non-reference discrete level and +1 increments for continuous attributes (relative to an implied baseline in the model, but no baseline is shown here).
  5. The Costs & Benefits tab updates after each scenario calculation and upon changing the QALY scenario (Low=0.02, Moderate=0.05, High=0.1). A summary table and bar chart detail total cost, QALYs, and net benefit.
  6. Scenarios can be saved, loaded, or exported to PDF, allowing you to compare multiple configurations for policy decisions.
