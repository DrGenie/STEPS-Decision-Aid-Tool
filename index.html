Introduction

Welcome to STEPS, the Scalable Training Estimation and Planning System. This tool is designed to assist stakeholders in making informed decisions about scaling Field Epidemiology Training Programs (FETP) in India by evaluating various training scenarios and analyzing their potential impact and costs.

Instructions

  1. Navigate through the tabs to understand the tool's functionality.
  2. In the Attributes & Descriptions tab, review the detailed attributes and their levels.
  3. Go to the Input tab to configure your desired training scenario by selecting different attribute levels.
  4. After setting your inputs, switch to the Results tab to view the predicted outcomes.
  5. Check the Predicted Uptake and Cost-Benefit Analysis tabs for detailed estimates.
  6. Use the Scenarios tab to save, compare, or export your configurations.

Attributes & Descriptions

Attribute Levels Description
Training Level Frontline (Basic), Intermediate, Advanced The complexity and depth of the training program.
Delivery Method In-Person, Online, Hybrid The mode through which training is delivered to participants.
Accreditation National, International, None The formal recognition of the training program by authoritative bodies.
Location of Training District-Level, State-Level, Regional Centers The geographical setting where training is conducted.
Cohort Size Small (10-20), Medium (21-50), Large (51+) The number of participants in each training batch.
Training Duration Short-Term (3-6 months), Medium-Term (7-12 months), Long-Term (13-24 months) The length of time participants spend in the training program.
Mentoring Approach In-Person, Virtual, None The method of providing guidance and support to trainees.
Cost per Participant Low (< ₹200,000), Medium (₹200,000 - ₹500,000), High (> ₹500,000) The financial investment required per trainee.
Curriculum Focus Infectious Diseases, Non-Communicable Diseases, One Health The primary areas of study within the training program.
