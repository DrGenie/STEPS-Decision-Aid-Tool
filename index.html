STEPS Decision Aid Tool

This tool supports decision makers in planning and scaling Field Epidemiology Training Programs (FETP) in India by predicting programme uptake, performing detailed cost–benefit analysis, and evaluating willingness‐to‐pay (WTP) for key features—all in USD.

Introduction

STEPS converts technical inputs into clear, actionable insights. It predicts participant uptake, computes cost–benefit outcomes, and evaluates the extra value (WTP) that programme features provide.

  • Instructions: Step-by-step guidance.
  • Attributes: Review programme features with icons and detailed tooltips showing reference levels.
  • Inputs: Configure your scenario using intuitive input cards.
  • WTP Results: View a narrow bar chart of extra value (in USD) for each attribute level (excluding the reference).
  • Predicted Program Uptake: See the estimated participation rate with tailored recommendations.
  • Costs & Benefits: Detailed analysis with breakdowns and educational explanations.
  • Scenarios: Save, compare, and export your configurations.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Review the Attributes tab to learn about each programme feature. Hover over an icon for detailed description and its reference level.
  2. In the Inputs tab, select your desired levels using input cards (each shows its reference) and adjust the sliders.
  3. Click "Calculate & View Results" to open a popup modal summarizing the predicted uptake and recommendations. (The full uptake chart is shown in the Uptake tab.)
  4. Examine the WTP Results tab to view a narrow bar chart for each non-reference attribute level along with a brief explanation of how WTP is calculated.
  5. Review the Costs & Benefits tab for a detailed breakdown of cost components (displayed as cards) and benefit calculations.
  6. Save your scenario in the Scenarios tab to compare configurations or export a PDF report.

Willingness to Pay (WTP) Results

WTP is calculated as the difference between an attribute level’s coefficient and its baseline divided by the (negative) cost coefficient, then scaled by 1000 to yield a USD value. Only non-reference levels are shown.

Costs & Benefits Analysis

Total costs = (Cohort Size × Training Cost). Fixed costs include local press ads, printing, postage, administrative personnel, trainer fees, on‑costs, and facilitator salaries. Variable costs include material costs, venue hire, session time, and travel. Benefits are estimated from QALY gains (assumed 0.05 per participant) multiplied by $50,000 per QALY.
