STEPS Decision Aid Tool

This tool helps decision makers plan and scale Field Epidemiology Training Programs (FETP) in India by predicting programme uptake, performing cost–benefit analyses, and evaluating willingness‐to‐pay (WTP) for six key attributes (all in USD).

Introduction

STEPS converts your inputs into clear, actionable insights. It predicts participant uptake, computes a cost–benefit summary, and evaluates extra value (WTP) for six attributes of an FETP.

  • Instructions: Step-by-step guidance.
  • Attributes: Overview of six attributes with reference levels.
  • Inputs: Select non-reference levels (radio buttons) and adjust the two sliders for continuous attributes.
  • WTP Results: A bar chart of extra value for each non-reference discrete level.
  • Predicted Program Uptake: Estimated uptake chart with dynamic suggestions.
  • Costs & Benefits: QALY scenario selection, summary table, bar chart, and cost breakdown cards.
  • Scenarios: Save, compare, or export your configurations to PDF.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Check Attributes to learn about each attribute’s reference baseline. Hover over icons for tips.
  2. In Inputs, select only non-reference options for the four discrete attributes. Adjust the two continuous sliders for Cohort Size and Cost per Participant.
  3. Click "Calculate & View Results" to see a modal with predicted uptake. A doughnut chart in Predicted Program Uptake also updates.
  4. WTP Results always shows a static chart for each non-reference discrete attribute level (independent of your scenario).
  5. Check Costs & Benefits for a dynamic summary, bar chart, and cost breakdown. Choose a QALY scenario: Low, Moderate, or High.
  6. In Scenarios, save or export multiple configurations to PDF.
