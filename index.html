STEPS Decision Aid Tool

This tool supports decision makers in planning and scaling Field Epidemiology Training Programs (FETP) in India. It predicts programme uptake, provides detailed cost–benefit analysis, and evaluates willingness‐to‐pay for key features.

STEPS helps you plan cost‐effective Field Epidemiology Training Programs by estimating participant uptake, calculating costs and benefits, and assessing willingness‐to‐pay for programme features.

  • Instructions – Step-by-step guidance.
  • Attributes – Review key programme features with icons and tooltips.
  • Inputs – Configure your scenario using intuitive controls.
  • WTP Results – See the extra value (in USD) assigned to each attribute level.
  • Predicted Program Uptake – View the estimated participation rate.
  • Costs & Benefits – Detailed analysis with educational summaries.
  • Scenarios – Save, compare, and export your configurations.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Review the Attributes tab to learn about each programme feature. Hover over icons for more info.
  2. In the Inputs tab, set your scenario using the level cards and sliders.
  3. Click "Calculate & View Results" to update all charts and summaries.
  4. Examine the WTP Results tab to see the monetary valuation for each attribute level relative to a baseline.
  5. Review the Predicted Program Uptake tab for an overview of expected participation.
  6. Check the Costs & Benefits tab for detailed cost–benefit outcomes and an educational explanation.
  7. Save and compare scenarios in the Scenarios tab, and export a PDF report if needed.

Costs & Benefits Analysis

This section shows the overall cost, benefit, and net benefit of your scenario. Fixed costs (e.g., trainer salaries, venue, materials) and variable costs (e.g., administrative expenses, travel) are combined. Benefits are estimated from QALY gains (assumed at 0.05 per participant) multiplied by $50,000 per QALY.
